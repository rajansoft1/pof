About Us

Parentof is a ‘child growth and development’ analytics company. Conceptualized in early 2014, Parentof was founded with a vision to ‘Simplify Parenting’ for young Indian parents by understanding and solving their unique problems arising from today’s day and age.

The company was formed when a diverse group of people with a collective experience of several decades, came together to study the patterns and challenges of parenting in the Indian society. The goal was simple – To leverage technology in ways that help parents to know their child better and experience better parenting.
